Guidelines for Patients

For Diabetes Care Patients

  • Navigation:
    1. Click on the Diabetes Care menu option in the navigation bar.
    2. You will be directed to the diabetes-specific page where you can enter your test results.
  • Input Instructions:
    1. Use your blood glucose monitor to measure your Blood Glucose Level and input the value in the provided field.
    2. Check your Body Mass Index (BMI) using a BMI calculator and input the value.
    3. From your fasting lipid profile, enter values for:
      • Total Cholesterol
      • HDL Cholesterol
      • LDL Cholesterol
      • Triglycerides
    4. Enter your HbA1c level from your test report.
    5. Double-check your entries to ensure they match your gadget's results before submission.
  • Submitting Data:
    1. Once all fields are filled, click the Submit button to get your prediction and recommendations.
    2. Review the recommendations displayed based on your inputs.

For Hypertension Care Patients

  • Navigation:
    1. Click on the Hypertension Care menu option in the navigation bar.
    2. You will be directed to the hypertension-specific page where you can input your results.
  • Input Instructions:
    1. Use a digital blood pressure monitor to measure your:
      • Systolic Blood Pressure (sysBP)
      • Diastolic Blood Pressure (diaBP)
    2. Measure your Heart Rate using your gadget and input the result.
    3. Use your BMI calculator to get your Body Mass Index and enter the value.
    4. Enter the following additional details:
      • Age
      • Whether you are a Current Smoker (yes/no).
      • The number of Cigarettes per Day (if applicable).
      • Whether you take BP Meds (yes/no).
      • Your Total Cholesterol and Glucose values from your lab results.
    5. Ensure all entries are accurate and match your gadget's or lab results.
  • Submitting Data:
    1. After completing the form, click the Submit button to view your prediction and receive actionable recommendations.
    2. Read the displayed recommendations to understand the next steps for managing your hypertension.
